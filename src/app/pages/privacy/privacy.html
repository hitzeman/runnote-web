Introduction

Welcome to RunNote. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data and tell you about your privacy rights.

Information We Collect

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you:

  • Identity Data including name and username
  • Contact Data including email address
  • Activity Data including running activities, distances, and times
  • Strava integration data when you connect your Strava account
  • Technical Data including IP address, browser type, and device information

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our running tracking service
  • To sync your activities with Strava
  • To analyze and improve our services
  • To communicate with you about updates and features
  • To ensure the security of our platform

Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. We limit access to your personal data to those who have a genuine business need to know it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request erasure of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data
  • Request transfer of your personal data
