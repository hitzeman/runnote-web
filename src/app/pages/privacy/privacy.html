1. Introduction

Welcome to RunNote. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our service to automatically analyze and enhance your Strava running activities with AI-powered workout summaries.

By using RunNote, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data from Strava

When you connect your Strava account to RunNote, we collect and process the following information:

  • Activity Data: Running activities including distance, duration, pace, splits, lap data, and heart rate information
  • Activity Descriptions: Existing descriptions on your Strava activities (read and write access to prepend AI-generated summaries)
  • Athlete Information: Your Strava athlete ID and basic profile information necessary for authentication
  • OAuth Tokens: Access and refresh tokens provided by Strava for authorized API access

2.2 Technical Data

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • IP addresses and device information when you access our service
  • Browser type and version
  • Webhook event data from Strava when new activities are created
  • Log data for service monitoring and error tracking

2.3 AI Processing Data

Your activity data (lap metrics, pace, heart rate) is sent to OpenAI's API for AI-powered analysis to generate workout classifications and summaries.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Workout Analysis: To analyze your running activities using AI and classify workouts (e.g., tempo runs, easy runs, interval workouts)
  • Activity Enhancement: To automatically generate and prepend smart workout summaries to your Strava activity descriptions
  • Strava Integration: To maintain authenticated access to your Strava account via OAuth tokens
  • Service Delivery: To process webhook events from Strava when you create new activities
  • Service Improvement: To monitor, maintain, and improve the functionality and accuracy of our AI analysis
  • Error Handling: To log and diagnose technical issues for service reliability

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or any purpose other than providing the RunNote service.

4. Third-Party Services

RunNote integrates with the following third-party services:

4.1 Strava

We connect to your Strava account using OAuth 2.0 authentication. Your use of Strava is governed by Strava's Privacy Policy. You can revoke RunNote's access to your Strava account at any time through your Strava account settings.

4.2 OpenAI

We use OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini API to analyze your workout data and generate intelligent summaries. Activity data (lap metrics, pace, heart rate) is sent to OpenAI for processing. OpenAI's use of this data is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy and API Data Usage Policies.

4.3 Microsoft Azure

Our infrastructure runs on Microsoft Azure. OAuth tokens and authentication data are stored in Azure Table Storage. Azure's handling of data is governed by Microsoft's Privacy Statement.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • Secure Storage: OAuth tokens are stored securely in Azure Table Storage with industry-standard encryption
  • Access Controls: Access to your data is limited to automated processes necessary for service delivery
  • Token Management: OAuth tokens are automatically refreshed and securely managed to maintain authorized access
  • HTTPS: All data transmission between your browser, our service, Strava, and OpenAI occurs over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Minimal Data: We only store what is necessary to provide the service (OAuth tokens and minimal athlete identification)

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your OAuth tokens and authentication data only as long as you maintain an active connection between RunNote and your Strava account. When you disconnect your Strava account or delete your RunNote authorization, we will delete your OAuth tokens and authentication data from our systems.

Activity data is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored in our systems. Generated summaries are written directly to your Strava activities and remain under your control on Strava.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (you can do this by disconnecting your Strava account)
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your personal data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing your personal data
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent at any time (you can revoke access through Strava settings)

To exercise these rights, please contact us or disconnect the RunNote application from your Strava account settings.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. Our infrastructure runs on Microsoft Azure, and data sent to OpenAI may be processed in the United States. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country. By using RunNote, you consent to these transfers.

9. Children's Privacy

RunNote is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when posted on this page.

11. How to Disconnect

You can disconnect RunNote from your Strava account at any time by:

  • Visiting your Strava account settings at strava.com/settings/apps
  • Finding "RunNote" in your authorized applications
  • Clicking "Revoke Access"

Upon disconnection, we will delete your OAuth tokens from our systems.
